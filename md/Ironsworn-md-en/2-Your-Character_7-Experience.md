# EXPERIENCE

When you *Fulfill Your Vow* [page 101](3-Moves_6-Quest-Moves.md#fulfill-your-vow), you gain **experience**. The amount of experience is based on the rank of the quest, and ranges from 1 to 5 points. Mark an ‘X’ on your character sheet for each point you’ve earned.

```text
EXPERIENCE
[X][X][X][X][O][O][O][O][O][O][O][O][O]
[O][O][O][O][O][O][O][O][O][O][O][O][O]
```

When you *Advance* [page 103](3-Moves_6-Quest-Moves.md#advance), you spend experience to purchase assets or upgrade an asset. Replace the ‘X’ for each point spent with a filled-in dot.

Per the *Advance* move, you may:
*   **Add a new asset** by spending 3 experience.
*   **Upgrade an asset** by spending 2 experience.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> To start, your experience is unmarked.
