# ASSETS

**Assets** are a key component of your character. They give you additional options and bonuses when making a move, and may include their own special moves. When you create your character [page 31](2-Your-Character_1-You-Are-Ironsworn.md), you select your starting assets. When you *Fulfill Your Vow* and gain experience [page 44](2-Your-Character_6-Assets.md#failing-an-asset-move), you can *Advance* to spend your experience on new assets or upgrade current assets.

To learn more about assets, see [page 39](file:///Users/ivanbiletskyi/Documents/Ivan/D&D/books/Ironsworn/Ironsworn-md/2-Your-Character.md).

> [!NOTE]
> You can mix-and-match assets however you like. There are no designated assets based on character classes or roles. However, you should avoid picking the same asset as another player.
