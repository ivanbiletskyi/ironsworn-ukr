# CHARACTER CREATION SUMMARY

Work through the following steps in whatever order you prefer.

1.  **Envision your character** [page 31](2-Your-Character_1-You-Are-Ironsworn.md#you-are-ironsworn).
2.  **Choose a name** [page 33](2-Your-Character_2-Character-Basics.md#character-basics).
3.  **Set your stats** by arranging these bonuses across edge, heart, iron, shadow, and wits in any order: 3, 2, 2, 1, 1 [page 33](2-Your-Character_2-Character-Basics.md#character-basics).
4.  **Set your health, spirit, and supply** to +5 [page 33](2-Your-Character_2-Character-Basics.md#character-basics).
5.  **Set your momentum** to +2, your **max momentum** to +10, and your **momentum reset** to +2 [page 35](2-Your-Character_3-Vows.md#vows).
6.  **Mark up to three background bonds** [page 36](2-Your-Character_4-Bonds.md#bonds).
7.  **Pick three assets** [page 39](2-Your-Character_6-Assets.md#assets).
8.  **Make note of any important equipment or items** [page 45](2-Your-Character_8-Equipment.md#equipment).
9.  **Set a background vow**, and give it a rank of extreme or epic. Then, envision your **inciting incident** and *Swear an Iron Vow*. For details on setting up your campaign, see page 193.
