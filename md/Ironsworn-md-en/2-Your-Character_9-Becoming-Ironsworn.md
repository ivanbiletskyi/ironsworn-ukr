# BECOMING IRONSWORN

In your first session, envision your current situation. Something has happened—or is about to happen—which puts you on the path of the Ironsworn. This is your **inciting incident**. Make it personal. Give it teeth. It’s not a situation you can just walk away from. You must set things right.

If you need inspiration for this starting situation, have a look at the prompts for vows in chapter 4 - ‘Your World’ [page 111](4-Your-World_1-Welcome-to-the-Ironlands.md#welcome-to-the-ironlands) and chapter 5 - ‘Foes and Encounters’ [page 133](5-Foes-and-Encounters_1-NPCs-in-the-Ironlands.md#npcs-in-the-ironlands). Or, you can *Ask the Oracle* [page 107](3-Moves_7-Fate-Moves.md#ask-the-oracle) and interpret the meaning of the answer.

To learn more about starting your campaign, see page 193. Then, *Swear an Iron Vow* and play to see what happens.
