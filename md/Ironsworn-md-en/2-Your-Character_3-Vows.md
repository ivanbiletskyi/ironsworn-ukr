# VOWS

When you *Swear an Iron Vow* [page 98](3-Moves_6-Quest-Moves.md#quest-moves), you give it a rank (troublesome, dangerous, formidable, extreme, or epic), and record it on your character sheet. You then use a vow progress track to mark when you *Reach a Milestone* [page 100](3-Moves_6-Quest-Moves.md#reach-a-milestone).

> [!IMPORTANT]
> You should start your first session with two vows: A long term goal (**your background vow**) and an immediate situation which must be dealt with (**your inciting incident**). You’ll find quest starters in chapter 4 - ‘Your World’ [page 111](4-Your-World_1-Welcome-to-the-Ironlands.md#welcome-to-the-ironlands) and chapter 5 - ‘Foes and Encounters’ [page 133](5-Foes-and-Encounters_1-NPCs-in-the-Ironlands.md#npcs-in-the-ironlands).
> 
> To learn more about your starting vows, see page 195.
