# GLOSSARY

This glossary contains all important terms of Ironsworn.

| Term | Ukrainian Term | Description |
|------|----------------|-------------|
| **Action Die** | Граник дії | A six-sided die (d6) rolled as part of an action roll, added to your stat and adds to determine your action score. |
| **Action Roll** | Кидок дії | A dice roll used to resolve risky or uncertain actions, consisting of two challenge dice (d10) and one action die (d6). |
| **Action Score** | Значення дії | The total of your action die plus your relevant stat and any adds, capped at 10. |
| **Adds** | Додаток | Bonuses that can be added to your action score from moves, assets, or specific circumstances. |
| **Allies** | Союзники | Other player characters in co-op or guided games who work together with your character. |
| **Advance** | Загартовуватись | A move where you spend 3 experience to add a new asset, or 2 experience to upgrade an existing asset. |
| **Aid Your Ally** | Допомогти союзнику | When you Secure an Advantage in direct support of an ally, they can take the benefits of that move instead of you. |
| **Ask the Oracle** | Спитати Оракула | A fate move used to resolve questions, discover details, determine character responses, or trigger encounters and events. |
| **Battle** | Поєдинок | A move used as an alternative to detailed combat scenes, rolling a stat based on your approach to fighting. |
| **Bolster Your Position** | Стати у стійку | In combat, a choice on a weak hit for the Clash move that gives you +2 momentum. |
| **Burden** | Тягар | A debility (cursed or tormented) that represents a life-changing experience binding you to a quest. |
| **Clash** | Відбитись | When your foe has initiative and you fight with them in close quarters, roll +iron to inflict your harm and potentially steal initiative. |
| **Compel** | Примушувати | When you attempt to persuade someone to do something through charm, threats, lies, or trickery. |
| **Companion Endure Harm** | Супутник зазнає шкоди | A move to determine outcome when your companion suffers physical damage. |
| **Dangerous** | Небезпечний | A challenge rank for moderate challenges; also a harm/stress rank (2 harm/stress). |
| **Draw a Conclusion** | Зробити висновки | An Ask the Oracle option where you decide the answer based on the most interesting and obvious result. |
| **Banes** | Згуба | Permanent debilities including maimed and corrupted that represent lasting physical or emotional trauma. |
| **Banner-Sworn** | Вірний стягові | An asset that requires having marked a bond with a leader or faction to acquire. |
| **Battle-Scarred** | Пошрамований | An asset that requires being maimed to acquire, representing survival through severe injury. |
| **End the Fight** | Завершити бій | A progress move used to resolve the outcome of a combat scene when you make a decisive action. |
| **Endure Harm** | Зазнати шкоди | When you face physical damage, suffer -health equal to your foe's rank or as appropriate to the situation. |
| **Endure Stress** | Зазнати стресу | When you face mental shock or despair, suffer -spirit equal to your foe's rank or as appropriate to the situation. |
| **Enter the Fray** | Увірватись в бій | When you enter into combat, set the rank of each foe and roll to determine who is initially in control. |
| **Epic** | Епічний | The highest challenge rank for legendary quests or foes; also a harm/stress rank (5 harm/stress). |
| **Extreme** | Екстремальний | A very high challenge rank for difficult quests or foes; also a harm/stress rank (4 harm/stress). |
| **Face Danger** | Стріти небезпеку | A catch-all move for risky or uncertain actions not covered by another move, rolling the appropriate stat. |
| **Face Death** | Стріти смерть | When you are brought to the brink of death and glimpse the world beyond, roll +heart to determine if death rejects you. |
| **Face Desolation** | Стріти відчай | When you are brought to the brink of desolation, roll +heart to determine if you resist and press on. |
| **Find an Opening** | Знайти прогалину | In combat, a choice on a strong hit for the Clash move that inflicts +1 harm. |
| **Foe Progress Track** | Шлях прогресу ворога | A progress track used to track damage inflicted on enemies in combat, marked based on harm dealt. |
| **Forge a Bond** | Скріпити стосунки | When you spend significant time with a person or community and create a connection, roll +heart to mark progress on your bond track. |
| **Formidable** | Грізний | A high challenge rank for difficult quests or foes; also a harm/stress rank (3 harm/stress). |
| **Forsake Your Vow** | Зректись присяги | When you renounce your quest, betray your promise, or lose a goal, clear the vow and suffer stress equal to its rank. |
| **Fulfill Your Vow** | Втілити присягу | A progress move used when you achieve what you believe to be the fulfillment of your vow, marking experience based on quest rank. |
| **Gather Information** | Зібрати інформацію | When you search an area, ask questions, conduct an investigation, or follow a track, roll +wits. |
| **Grant First Strike** | Право першого удару | In combat, a choice on a weak hit for the Draw the Circle move where your foe has initiative. |
| **Heal** | Лікувати | When you treat an injury or ailment, roll +wits (or +iron if lower) to restore health. |
| **Hold No Iron** | Лишитись без заліза | In combat, a choice on a weak hit for the Draw the Circle move where you suffer +1 harm. |
| **Inciting Incident** | Спонукальний інцидент | An event that triggers a new vow during campaign setup or character creation. |
| **Initiative** | Ініціатива | In combat, having the advantage of acting first in a round, determined by move outcomes and choices. |
| **Journey Progress Track** | Шлях прогресу подорожі | A progress track used to measure headway toward a destination during travel. |
| **Make Camp** | Розмістити табір | When you rest and recover for several hours in the wild, roll +supply to regain health, spirit, or momentum. |
| **Mark Progress** | Відмітити прогрес | Filling in progress boxes on a progress track based on successful moves during quests, journeys, or combat. |
| **Milestone** | Проміжна віха | A significant achievement or obstacle overcome in a quest that allows you to mark progress on your quest track. |
| **Out of Supply** | Зазнати злиднів | When your supply is exhausted (reduced to 0), mark unprepared and suffer consequences. |
| **Pack** | Ватага | A group of foes combined into a single progress track for easier management in combat. |
| **Pay the Price** | Сплатити ціну | A move triggered on a miss to determine negative outcomes and complications. |
| **Progress Move** | Ходи прогресу | A type of move that uses challenge dice compared to your progress score to resolve extended challenges. |
| **Reach a Destination** | Досягти місця призначення | A progress move used when your journey comes to an end, rolling challenge dice against your journey progress score. |
| **Reach a Milestone** | Досягти проміжної віхи | When you make significant progress in your quest by overcoming obstacles, you may mark progress on your quest track. |
| **Resupply** | Поповнити припаси | When you hunt, forage, or scavenge in the field, roll +wits to increase your supply track. |
| **Secure an Advantage** | Здобути перевагу | When you assess a situation, make preparations, or attempt to gain leverage, roll the appropriate stat to gain momentum or position. |
| **Sojourn** | Співіснування | When you spend time in a community seeking assistance, roll +heart to clear debilities, recover status tracks, or gain quests. |
| **Strike** | Напасти | When you have initiative and attack in close quarters, roll +iron to inflict harm. |
| **Swear an Iron Vow** | Дати залізну присягу | When you swear upon iron to complete a quest, write your vow, give it a rank, and roll +heart to determine your starting momentum. |
| **Test Your Bond** | Випробувати стосунки | When your bond is tested through conflict, betrayal, or circumstance, roll +heart to determine if the relationship strengthens or breaks. |
| **To the Death** | До смерті | In combat, a choice on a weak hit for the Draw the Circle move where the fight must end with death. |
| **Troublesome** | Клопітний | The lowest challenge rank for simple challenges; also a harm/stress rank (1 harm/stress). |
| **Turn the Tide** | Кинутись стрімголов | Once per fight, a move where you risk it all to steal initiative and make a move with +1 momentum. |
| **Undertake a Journey** | Вирушити в подорож | When you travel across hazardous or unfamiliar lands, set journey rank and roll +wits to reach waypoints and mark progress. |
| **Waypoint** | Орієнтир | A feature of the landscape, settlement, or point-of-interest reached during a journey segment. |
